Bachelor of Business Administration

BBA - 3 Year Degree Program

The Bachelor of Business Administration (B.B.A.) is a 3 year degree course, designed to provide a clear understanding of the business oriented concepts. The objective of BBA course is to build competence in students to effectively undertake entry level managerial jobs in organisations and to develop various soft skills required for the profession. BBA course allows a student to enter the diverse field of finance and management. The program also serves as a stepping-stone for higher career programs like MBA, CA, ICWA, CS, ICFA etc.

Eligibility & Course Details

Eligibility for Admission

Applicants are required to have passed Plus Two or equivalent examination with not less than 50% marks in aggregate.

Course Duration

3 Year Course (Total 6 Semesters)

Merit Calculation

Marks obtained in Part III of plus two or equivalent less handicap marks with 50% quota for science group plus weightage mark if any.

Bachelor of Computer Applications

BCA - 3 Year Degree Program

The Department of Computer Application offers a 3-year degree programme in Computer Applications (BCA). Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is an undergraduate program where students are exposed to various areas of Computer Applications including the latest developments in the industry. Department has well qualified and vibrant faculty dedicated to train the students. Most of the students passed out are well placed or have opted for higher studies. The department has a well-equipped computer lab with latest computers, software and high speed internet. The department ensures that program objectives are constantly met and learning outcomes are monitored through periodical tests and assignments. Every year, on an average 60% of the students are getting placement through various campus recruitments. The department encourages students to get funded projects and we have a set of completed and on-going projects. The department also undertakes website development as consultancy service.

Eligibility & Course Details

Eligibility for Admission

Candidates shall be required to have passed the Plus Two/equivalent examination in science stream with Mathematics/Computer Science/Informatics as optional.

Course Duration

3 Year Course (Total 6 Semesters)

Merit Calculation

Total Marks obtained in Part III less handicap marks obtained for Maths/Computer Science Plus Weightage mark if any.

Bachelor of Commerce

B.Com - 3 Year Degree Program

The department of Commerce offers four degree programmes - B.com Finance & Taxation, B.com Computer Application, B.com Co-Operation and B.com Marketing. The department has well qualified and trained faculty capable of imparting best training to the students. We have a well equipped computer LAB and very good Library to support the students. The department conducts Commerce Fest every year to enrich the talents of the students. Apartfrom classroom training, the students get opportunity to have hands on training through various projects and programmes.The department is eagerly waiting for approval from MG University to start M. Com Course

Eligibility & Course Details

Eligibility

Admission to the B.com Degree Programmes shall be open only candidates who have passed the plus two or equivalent examination recognized by the University with Book Keeping and Accountancy and any two of the following subjects viz (1) Commerce 2) Commercial Correspondence and Commercial Geography (3) Eco-nomics (4) Life Insurance with Salesmanship (5) Barik-ing with Secretarial Practice (6) Business Studies (7) Mathematics (8) Computer Science Computer Applica-tions (9) Informatics Practice and management (10) Informatics Practice (11) Management (12) Accoun-tancy as optional under part Iii of the examination OR Who have passed Plus Two or equivalent examination recognized by the University with other subjects under part iii optional provided they have secured 45% of the aggregate

Course Duration

3 Year Course (Total 6 Semesters)
